INVESTING ALPHA TO OMEGA - YOUR INVESTMENT JOURNEY

Please put 20th October in your diary to join us for the morning at our London Office for the JLT Employee Benefits Investment Conference.

The theme for the conference will be “Investing: Alpha to Omega – Your Investment Journey”.

Why you should attend

The agenda has been designed to articulate some of the current important topics concerning today’s Pension Schemes and how they structure their portfolios, from both JLT and a carefully selected group of investment partners.

Brief agenda

8:30am Registration and coffee:

9:00am Welcome address and presentations considering

Alternative Sources for Growth - The changing role of equity allocation and how to access alternative sources of growth to complement your existing growth assets

The world beyond LDI: End Game Planning - Looking at the different stages a portfolio would go through on the way to a buyout

How to Hedge your Bets - Looking at the stabilising section of your portfolio, including positioning on the route to buyout

1:00pm Lunch

The main aim of the day is to provide relevant and useful information, with the opportunity to ask questions of the panels after each of the sessions, as well as during the coffee break and lunch. More details to follow.

Attendance is free for pension scheme trustees and invited guests.

If you would like to talk to the investment consulting team in advance of the conference, or are unable to attend and would like further information or a copy of the slide pack after the event, please contact Head of Investment Consulting, John Breedon at @john_breedon@jltgroup.com.
